About me

01

I'm Anushka Thakur, a third-year B.Tech student in Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, at VIT Bhopal University.

My core interest lies in Machine Learning and Data Science — I enjoy working through the entire process of building ML models, from understanding and preprocessing data to training, evaluating, and improving model performance.

Alongside my coursework, I'm building a foundation in web development, learning how intelligent systems can be integrated into real, usable applications rather than staying confined to notebooks and experiments.

I'm comfortable working with Python, C++, and Java, and I use tools like Git, GitHub, Google Colab, and Jupyter Notebook in my day-to-day learning and projects.

I consider myself a quick learner who enjoys tackling new problems, whether that means picking up an unfamiliar concept, debugging a tricky piece of code, or collaborating with a team toward a shared goal.

I'm currently maintaining a CGPA of 8.64, and I actively look for opportunities to contribute to open-source projects and expand my practical experience — including being part of GirlScript Summer of Code (GSSoC) 2026.

Outside of academics, I'm a member of the Fashion Society at VIT Bhopal, which has helped me build confidence, communication, and teamwork skills that go beyond the technical side of things.

What drives me is curiosity — about how things work, how data tells stories, and how AI is shaping the corporate and real-world landscape. I'm excited to keep learning, keep building, and eventually contribute meaningfully to the world of AI and data-driven decision-making.
